How to use the web to view Wandile's Aircraft Collection:

At the top of the page, you can view airliners by the manufacturers. When you
click on  manufacturer, you will open a page with the corresponding models for
the manufacturer.

The Browse page:

Browse for aircraft by their attributes:
Number of engines, number of seats, range, operators and body size.
Select an option from a drop down list and click the 'Select' button.
The content on the page will then change to show the aircraft matching the
attribute selected.
